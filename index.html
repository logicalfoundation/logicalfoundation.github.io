Welcome to the Logical Foundation

We are a non-profit collective of, by and for students, common people, and volunteers passionate about science, philosophy, and logic. We are committed to improving the lives of Bangladeshi people.

Our Mission

  • Spread awareness about science, logic & critical thinking to combat fallacies, illiteracy & misinformation.
  • Promote reasoned discourse and intellectual integrity through educational programs, research, and community outreach.
